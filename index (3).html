Clean water is not a luxury.

2.2 billion people lack access to safely managed drinking water. We build modular RO containers that bring industrial-grade purification to any location — coastal, rural or urban — without fixed infrastructure.

Food should be produced close to where it is eaten.

Italy imports almost all of its salmon. The Netherlands ships vegetables thousands of kilometres. Zaffirè RAS and CEA containers close that gap — enabling local, traceable, fresh food production anywhere there is space for a shipping container.

Technology must serve communities, not replace them.

Industry 5.0 is not about removing people from the process. It is about giving communities the tools to feed themselves, manage their water and build resilience — with human wellbeing at the centre of every design decision.

Industry 5.0

Beyond automation.
Towards purpose.

The European Commission defines Industry 5.0 as a new paradigm for industrial production — one that moves beyond efficiency and profit to place human wellbeing, planetary boundaries and societal resilience at the centre of innovation.

Industry 4.0 gave us automation, artificial intelligence and the Internet of Things. Industry 5.0 asks a deeper question: automation for what? The answer is a transition from shareholder value to stakeholder value — prosperity that respects our planet and serves our communities.

Das AquaVita is an Industry 5.0 company. Every Zaffirè container is designed with three non-negotiable values: it must sustain the planet, it must serve the people around it, and it must remain resilient under pressure. These are not marketing commitments — they are engineering requirements.

The Zaffirè System

One platform.
Three solutions.

Each Zaffirè unit is a self-contained, deployable module — available in 20FT and 40FT formats. No fixed infrastructure required. Connect water, connect power, start producing. Our containers are engineered in the Netherlands and deployed wherever they are needed most.

💧
RO System Zaffirè RO
💧 Reverse Osmosis · Water Purification

Clean water.
From any source.

The Zaffirè RO container delivers industrial-grade reverse osmosis water purification in a deployable unit. From coastal desalination to river-source treatment, from emergency response to permanent community water supply — no fixed infrastructure required.

  • Operates on any water source — river, seawater, brackish, borehole
  • Full membrane filtration removing bacteria, heavy metals and chemical contaminants
  • Available in 20FT and 40FT formats — scalable output for any community size
  • Remote IoT monitoring and real-time water quality data
  • Deployable in under 72 hours — no civil works, no fixed connection
  • Aligned with EU Drinking Water Directive and WHO quality standards

RAS System Zaffirè RAS
🐟 Recirculating Aquaculture · Land-Based

Local fish.
No ocean required.

The Zaffirè RAS container produces premium seafood in a closed-loop recirculating system. Atlantic salmon, rainbow trout, tilapia, sea bass and prawns — grown on land, without antibiotics, without the sea, without compromise. Italy imports nearly all the fish it consumes. Zaffirè RAS changes that equation.

  • Closed-loop water recirculation — 95% water efficiency vs open-pen aquaculture
  • Zero antibiotic use — water quality managed by precision filtration and AI monitoring
  • Fully traceable from egg to plate — ideal for premium retail and food service
  • Species-flexible: salmon, trout, tilapia, sea bass, sea bream, prawns
  • Integrates with CEA units — fish waste becomes plant nutrition (circular design)
  • Land-based production eliminates sea lice, escape risk and marine pollution
Enquire about the Zaffirè RAS →
🌿
CEA System Zaffirè CEA
🌿 Controlled Environment Agriculture · Precision Growing

Saffron. Herbs.
Year-round. Anywhere.

The Zaffirè CEA container is a precision-controlled indoor growing unit producing high-value crops 365 days a year. LED lighting tuned to each crop's photosynthetic spectrum. Climate systems maintaining optimal temperature, humidity and CO₂. Saffron — the world's most valuable spice — grown with complete consistency in a shipping container.

  • Full climate control — temperature, humidity, CO₂ and photoperiod managed to within 0.1°C
  • LED spectrum optimised per crop — from vegetative growth to flowering induction
  • Up to 48 growing cycles per year for fast-growing microgreens and herbs
  • Saffron production — one of the highest-value crops in the world — in any climate
  • Hydroponic or substrate growing systems — configurable per customer requirement
  • Integrates with RAS unit — uses nutrient-rich fish water as fertiliser input

Technology

Where Industry 4.0 tools
serve Industry 5.0 purpose.

🤖

AI-driven water management

Machine learning models monitor water quality across every sensor in real time — detecting anomalies before they affect production, optimising filtration cycles and predicting maintenance needs weeks in advance.

📡

IoT sensor networks

Each container is a dense sensor node — pH, dissolved oxygen, temperature, conductivity, turbidity, light intensity and atmospheric parameters — feeding continuous data to our cloud platform and to research partners.

🔄

Circular by design

RAS wastewater becomes fertiliser for the CEA unit. LED heat is recovered for climate management. Water that enters a Zaffirè system is used, recirculated and returned to the environment cleaner than it arrived.

🌐

Horizon Europe integration

Zaffirè containers are nodes in the EU Source2Sea and Digital Twin Ocean programmes. Every deployment contributes real production and environmental data to European-scale research on sustainable food and water systems.

📊

Remote operations dashboard

Operators monitor and control every container from anywhere in the world. Full telemetry, alert systems and automated responses — a single team can manage a distributed fleet of containers across multiple countries.

🛡️

EU data sovereignty

All data is hosted on EU sovereign cloud infrastructure, compliant with GDPR and the EU Data Governance Act. Sensor data, production records and environmental monitoring remain under full EU jurisdiction.

Contributing to the
EU Digital Twin Ocean

Every Zaffirè container deployed in the Mediterranean region is a live data node for the EU Digital Twin Ocean — Europe's ambition to create a high-fidelity digital replica of all European seas.

Our Source2Sea Horizon Europe application (OCEAN-02) connects the source of aquatic pollution — land-based agriculture and aquaculture — directly to its marine impact. Zaffirè containers close the loop: they produce food cleanly, and they measure what flows from land to sea.

How it works

From Leeuwarden
to the
Mediterranean.

Every Zaffirè deployment follows the same four-step process — from engineering and QA in the Netherlands to live production and data contribution in the field.

Partner with us →
01
🇳🇱
Design and engineering — Leeuwarden
Zaffirè containers are designed, engineered and quality-assured at our WaterCampus base in Leeuwarden, Netherlands. Working alongside Wetsus — the world's leading water technology research centre — we apply the latest advances in water treatment, aquaculture biology and precision agriculture to every unit before it leaves the factory.
02
🚢
Deployment on site — Italy and beyond
Containers are shipped to the deployment location. Our technical team manages site preparation, container placement and system commissioning. Current active sites are in Gargano and Taranto, Puglia — with expansion planned across Italian coasts, including Sicily, Sardinia, Liguria and the Veneto region. No civil works, no planning delay, no fixed infrastructure dependency.
03
⚡
Connect and produce — within 72 hours
Connect to a water source and a power supply. The container calibrates automatically. RO units begin producing purified water within hours. RAS units are stocked and begin the growing cycle within days. CEA units initiate their first growing cycle within 48 hours of commissioning. Each container comes with full operator training, remote monitoring access and 24/7 technical support.
04
📡
Monitor, scale and contribute data
Real-time sensor data flows to our operations dashboard and to European research programmes including the EU Digital Twin Ocean and Source2Sea Horizon Europe initiative. As your production grows, additional containers can be deployed and integrated into the same management system. Each new site strengthens the network and contributes to the collective intelligence of the platform.
