Why it matters

Water and food systems are broken

Centralised, chemical-dependent infrastructure cannot scale to meet 21st-century demand. Agriculture consumes 70% of global freshwater — inefficiently. 40% of European water bodies fail ecological standards. An integrated, deployable solution has never existed.

Zaffiré changes that. We have turned Dutch-standard water and food security into a modular system the rest of the world can deploy in days, not decades.

How it works

One unit.
Clean water.
Fresh food.
Zero waste.

Zaffiré integrates three proven technologies into a single, closed-loop modular platform. Each system feeds the next — nothing is wasted, everything is optimised by AI in real time.

>95% Water reuse rate
Near-zero external discharge
RO

Pure Water

Multi-stage reverse osmosis delivering ultrapure water for drinking, irrigation, and industrial use. Removes salinity, chemicals, and pathogens to WHO standards.

RAS

Aquaculture

Recirculating aquaculture system growing tilapia, salmon, trout, and prawns in fully controlled conditions. Nutrient-rich effluent feeds the grow containers — nothing exits the loop.

CEA

Crops

Controlled-environment agriculture with precision LED lighting. Saffron, microgreens, and herbs grown year-round, independent of climate, season, or soil quality.

AI

Intelligent Closed Loop

Predictive maintenance, automated dosing, and 24/7 remote monitoring. Every container continuously learns. Water returns to RO — the loop closes, waste disappears.
